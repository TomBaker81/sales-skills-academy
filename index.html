Sales Training

The core skills every call draws on — opening well, asking questions that actually uncover something, handling pushback without getting defensive, positioning the fix in the customer's terms, and knowing when an opportunity is genuinely worth pursuing. Work through these before the Focus Area Playbooks and the virtual call — they're the "why" behind both.

Focus Area Playbooks

Pick a focus area below to learn what it is and why it matters, then practice a guided discovery path — Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-payoff — to see how you'd qualify a real conversation. If an area turns out to be a genuine dead end, you'll get the option to cross-sell into a different one, just like a real call rarely ends after one "no."
